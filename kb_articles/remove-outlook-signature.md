# How to Remove a Custom Email Signature in Outlook

## Steps

1. Open Outlook
2. Click **File** → **Options** → **Mail**
3. Under "Compose messages," click **Signatures...**
4. In the "Select signature to edit" box, click the signature you want to remove
5. Click **Delete**
6. Click **OK** to close the Signatures window
7. Click **OK** to close Outlook Options

Your custom signature has been removed.
