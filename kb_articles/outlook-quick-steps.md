# Creating a Quick Step in Outlook Classic

## Overview
This guide shows how to create a Quick Step in Outlook Classic that adds pre-defined text to a new email body without setting recipients or subject.

## Steps

### 1. Open Quick Steps Panel
- Go to the **Home** tab in Outlook
- Click **Create New** in the Quick Steps section
- [SCREENSHOT: Quick Steps section on Home ribbon]

### 2. Configure the Quick Step
- In the **Name** field, enter a descriptive name for your Quick Step
- Under **Actions**, click the dropdown and select **New Message**
- [SCREENSHOT: Edit Quick Step dialog box]

### 3. Add Body Text
- Click **Show Options**
- In the **Text** field, enter your email body content
- Leave **To** and **Subject** fields empty
- [SCREENSHOT: Expanded options showing Text field]

### 4. Set Keyboard Shortcut (Optional)
- Choose a shortcut key from the **Shortcut key** dropdown
- [SCREENSHOT: Shortcut key dropdown]

### 5. Save
- Click **Finish**
- Your Quick Step now appears in the ribbon

## Usage
- Click the Quick Step button, or use your keyboard shortcut
- A new email opens with your pre-filled body text
- Add recipients and subject manually
